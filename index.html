Hazrat Ali ka Janam

  • Hazrat Ali ka janam 13 Rajab 600 CE (Islamic calendar ke mutabiq 23 BH) ko Makkah, Arabia mein hua.
  • Unke walid ka naam Abu Talib ibn Abdul-Muttalib tha, aur walida ka naam Bibi Fatimah bint Asad tha.
  • Unka janam Quraysh qabeela ke ek mashhoor khandan mein hua.
  • Hazrat Ali ka janam ghar mein hua, jahan unki maan Bibi Fatimah bint Asad ne unhe janm diya.

Hazrat Ali ki Kitni Biwiyan Thi

    Hazrat Ali ki kitni biwiyan thi, yeh jankari sahih taur par pesh nahi ki gayi hai, lekin unki zyada mashhoor biwiyan chaar thi:
  1. Bibi Fatimah bint Muhammad: Unki shadi Hazrat Ali aur Bibi Fatimah (Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم ki beti) ke darmiyan hui.
  2. Umm ul-Banin bint Hizam: Unki shadi Umm ul-Banin ke saath hui.
  3. Asma bint Umais: Unki shadi Asma bint Umais ke saath hui.
  4. Hanane bint Abi Talib: Unki shadi Hanane bint Abi Talib ke saath hui.
